A predictive disease project

This machine learning system can diagnose two acute inflammations of bladder: 'Inflammation of Urinary Bladder' and 'Nephritis of Renal Pelvis Origin'. This medical dataset truly needs privacy! Because we cannot divulge the sexually-transmitted diseases of patients. So, what we learned about PySyft and OpenMined is applied in this project. Federated learning will protect the privacy of datasets in each hospital and at the same time, a more robust machine learning model will benefit all hospitals.
